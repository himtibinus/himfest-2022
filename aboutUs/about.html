ABOUT US

Background

TECHFEST 2022 is a competition event initiated by HIMTI Binus University with the aim of providing a forum for creativity and competition for all students in Indonesia.
This year, TECHFEST has the theme "Building Adaptive, Collaborative and Innovative Gen Z Tech Talent" and will be held in a hybrid manner at Binus University @Bandung.

History

The Tech Festival (TECHFEST) was first held in Malang under the name "HIMTI Festival" in 2021 online due to the Covid-19 pandemic. However, this did not dampen the enthusiasm of the participants to be able to participate and enliven the series of HIMTI Festival 2021 activities.

About Logo

In the TECHFEST 2022 logo, there are elements that support each other, namely Puzzle and Brain. The puzzle symbolizes the goal of TECHFEST 2022, which is to create a forum for collaboration for the committee and participants. The brain symbolizes that every human being has innovation to solve a problem. Color gradations symbolize adaptive humans who can adapt to all new changes for the better. and the purple color is obtained from the combination of blue and pink as a form of adaptive.
