TECH FESTIVAL 2022

TECHFEST 2022 or Tech Festival 2022 is an IT festival event that is annually held by HIMTI Binus University.

This year's TECHFEST theme is "Building Adaptive, Collaborative and Innovative Gen Z Tech Talent". The theme was taken with the aim of being able to help and invite students and the wider community to develop together so that they can become a superior generation, especially in the field of technology.

TECHFEST 2022 is holding three main events: Competition, TechTalk (seminar), and TechExpo (technology exhibition)

TECHFEST 2022 will be held onsite except Competition, which will be held online.

Two competitions will be held in TECHFEST 2022: Mobile Development and UI/UX Design

Participants are allowed to register in several competitions, but for the team leader it can only be in one of the competitions.

The number of participants per team is 1-3 people

All team members must come from the same university, but the majors may be different.

Registration can be done through the registration page at www.techfest.himtibinus.or.id

Yes, the registration fee for each competition is Rp 75.000,00
